# Flash the ESP32 controller (in your browser)

1. Hub → **Devices → Flash**
2. Connect an **ESP32-S3** (native USB recommended)
3. Click **Flash “Diff-Drive + ToF”**

After reboot, the device advertises and binds automatically to starter flows.
